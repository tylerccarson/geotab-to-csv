Google Drive Datafeed

Get a .csv copy of your fleet synced to your Google Drive

1) Enter an email that has an associated Google account

2) It is VERY IMPORTANT that after opening the link send your your email, that you add the shared folder to your own drive

3) Download Google Drive Backup and Sync (free service)

4) Open up Google Drive and you should be able to watch a .csv file of your fleet update on your machine every 10 minutes

Source code provided as part of the Geotab SDK.
